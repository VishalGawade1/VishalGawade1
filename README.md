## 🧠 Who I Am

> I am a **domain authority for AI training and evaluation pipelines** for large language models.

I define what *high-quality* training data looks like and how model quality should be measured. Day to day I sit at the intersection of **researchers, scientists, and the data that makes models better** — designing evaluation frameworks, building golden/benchmark datasets, and steering **human-in-the-loop (HITL)** and **model-in-the-loop (MITL)** workflows that raise both data quality and model performance. I do this at **Amazon (AGI Data Services)**, where I also mentor junior researchers and reviewers and stay on the front edge of GenAI / LLM advances. Outside the day job, I open pull requests against the open-source tools I depend on.
